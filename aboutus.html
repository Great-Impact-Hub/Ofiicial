MBSG Around the world

It started in 1999 with a scouting mission to Italy and continues annually every summer and sometimes during the winter. Its goal is to learn about other cultures and develop a spirit of tolerance and brotherhood by participating in international camps, with participants ranging from 1,000 to 40,000 scouts and guides from around the world.

Scout Activities

In addition to its annual summer trips and scout camping, Misr Baladna has regular scout meetings for each age group and organizes trips inside Egypt to allow them to discover their country and other scout branches inside and outside Egypt.
